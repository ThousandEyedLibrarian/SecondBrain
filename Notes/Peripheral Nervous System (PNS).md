Comprised of the peripheral nerves and ganglia and the sensory receptors.

Somatic: Nerves connected to voluntary skeletal [[Muscle]]s and sensory receptors.
Uses:
- Afferent nerve fibers (incoming): [[Axon]]s that carry info away from the periphery to the [[Central Nervous System (CNS)]]
- Efferent nerve fibers (outgoing): Vice versa

Autonomic: Nerves that connect to the heart, blood vessels, smooth muscles, and glands
